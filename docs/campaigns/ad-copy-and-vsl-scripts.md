## Recording Guidelines

- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- **Lighting:** Soft, natural lighting on face
- **Background:** Clean, professional (home office or neutral)
- **Delivery:** Conversational, education-first tone (not salesy)
- **Eye contact:** Look at camera, not script
- **Pace:** Moderate, pause for emphasis on key points
