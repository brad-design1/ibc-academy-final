## RECORDING PRODUCTION NOTES

### Technical Specifications:
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p) minimum
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Audio:** 48kHz, no background noise
- **File Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Max File Size:** 100MB per video

### Visual Setup Options:
**Option A: Professional Office**
- Bookshelves in background
- Desk with clean surface
- Good natural or ring lighting

**Option B: Clean Background**
- Solid wall or backdrop
- Professional lighting
- No distractions

**Option C: Branded Background**
- IBC Academy logo subtle in background
- Professional but approachable
- Consistent with brand colors

### Delivery Style:
- Look slightly off-camera (not directly at lens)
- Speak conversationally, not performatively
- Use hand gestures naturally
- Smile at beginning and end
- Pause for emphasis on key numbers (34.5%, $50,000, etc.)

### Post-Production (if editing):
- Add captions/subtitles (80% watch without sound)
- Lower third with name/credentials
- Simple zoom in on key moments
- Clean cuts between sections if needed
